Our Story

Born from the belief that
the world's best founders
are building here.

IMEA Venture Capital was founded in Dubai in 2018 by a team of former investment bankers, operators, and founders who had seen firsthand how underserved the region's entrepreneurs were by global capital.

We set out to build a different kind of venture firm — one that combines the analytical rigor of institutional finance with the on-the-ground fluency that only comes from living and working in the markets we invest in. Six years and $2.1B later, we remain committed to that founding principle.

How We Think

Investment
Philosophy

Our approach is built on four interlocking principles that guide every decision from first meeting to final exit.

01 Thesis-Driven

We invest in inevitabilities, not trends.

Before any deal, we form a long-form investment thesis on the sector, the timing, and the structural tailwinds at play. We do not chase deal flow — we create it by publishing conviction before it becomes consensus.

02 Operator-Led

We have built companies. We know what hard looks like.

Every partner on our team has either founded, scaled, or operated a business in an emerging market. That experience is what separates advice that sounds right from advice that actually works on the ground.

03 Network as Moat

Our portfolio companies benefit from each other.

We actively engineer collaboration between our portfolio companies — shared customers, joint ventures, talent referrals. The IMEA network is a strategic asset that compounds in value as we grow.

04 Responsible Capital

Profit and purpose are not in conflict.

Every investment is evaluated through an ESG lens. We believe the most durable businesses are those that solve real problems for real communities — and that responsible investing generates superior risk-adjusted returns over time.
